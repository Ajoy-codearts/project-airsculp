Awake the Entire Time

Elite Body Sculpture never uses general anesthesia, meaning patients remain awake throughout the entire procedure listening to music or chatting with a friend, nurse, or surgeons Using local anesthesia drastically reduces risks while also allowing patients to be maneuvered during treatment to ensure perfectly balanced results.

Rather than cutting large incisions with a scalpel, AirSculpt® uses a biopsy punch tool just two milimeters wide to create an entryway that heals with dissolvable sutures.
